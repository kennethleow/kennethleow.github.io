Kenneth Leow: Bringing Stories to Life on Screen

With over a decade in the video and film industry, Kenneth has a knack for turning creative sparks into compelling narratives. His experience spans continents, connecting talented teams in Malaysia, Singapore, and Australia.

Social media content is his playground. Whether it's YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram, Kenneth guides productions from concept to completion, ensuring every frame resonates. He doesn't just produce, he directs, he collaborates, always keeping the audience at the heart of the story.

His legacy? Helping pioneer social video content in Malaysia. Working with SAYS.com, he paved the way for a wave of creators, inspiring platforms like World of Buzz and TRP to join the conversation.

Today, Kenneth doesn't just create content, he builds partnerships. He connects teams with the right resources, making sure every production has the wind in its sails. So, if you're looking for a seasoned collaborator to bring your vision to life, Kenneth's your guide.
